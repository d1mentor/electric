What we offer:

Execution of works of any complexity. We employ only qualified specialists with specialized education and the necessary permits. We cooperate with both private clients and real estate developers. We take contracts for large-scale works. We perform the work efficiently and on time. We work in the city of Wroclaw and the surrounding area. Consider the possibility of business trips to Poland and the European Union.

We perform the following types of work:

electrical installation

Electrical installation in apartment and private residential buildings, offices, shops and industrial premises.

installation of electrical panels

Complete set and installation of electrical and multimedia teletechnical boards, as well as electricity metering boards.

measurements

Measurements of the impedance of the short circuit loop, insulation resistance, protective shutdown device, lighting.

installation of photovoltaic panels

Installation of lightning protection systems for private and multi-apartment residential buildings, offices, shopping centers and industrial enterprises.

installation of grounding

Installation of grounding and potential equalization systems for private residential buildings.

installation of security systems

Installation of security systems, access control systems and video surveillance.

installation of computer networks

Installation of computer networks, configuration of routers and network interaction of computers.

installation of smart home systems

Installation of home automation systems "smart home" and industrial automation systems.
